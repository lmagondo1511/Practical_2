Background & Biography

Ashleigh Hulett is a third-year Informatics student at the University of Pretoria with a strong passion for software development and cybersecurity.

She enjoys coding projects that challenge her problem-solving abilities and allow her to explore new programming languages. She is an active member of the university’s Tech Society, where she participates in hackathons and cybersecurity workshops. Ashleigh aspires to build a career in cybersecurity, specializing in ethical hacking and digital forensics.

Hobbies & Interests

Outside of her academic pursuits, Ashleigh is an avid rock climber. She enjoys both indoor and outdoor climbing, finding that the sport challenges her physically and mentally. Climbing has taught her resilience and strategic thinking, skills that she applies in her approach to software development and cybersecurity.

Ashleigh is also passionate about music and plays the guitar in her free time. She sees a connection between music and coding, as both require creativity, precision, and problem-solving. When she’s not climbing or coding, Ashleigh enjoys exploring new cybersecurity tools and staying up to date with the latest advancements in ethical hacking and digital forensics.
