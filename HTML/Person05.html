Background & Biography

Unathi Ledwaba is a third-year Informatics student at the University of Pretoria with a passion for software engineering and cloud computing.

She enjoys tackling complex technical challenges and has worked on projects involving backend infrastructure and web development. As an active member of the university’s Tech Society, she participates in coding challenges and collaborative development projects. Unathi aspires to work in cloud architecture and DevOps, helping to advance cloud-based technologies.

Hobbies & Interests

Beyond her academic interests, Unathi is a dedicated long-distance runner who frequently competes in marathons. She finds that endurance training builds patience and perseverance, qualities that also enhance her programming skills.

In her free time, she enjoys playing strategy-based video games and staying up to date with emerging cloud technologies.
