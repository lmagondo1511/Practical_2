Background & Biography

Ntsika Mazamisa is a third-year Informatics student at the University of Pretoria with a strong passion for artificial intelligence and data science. She specializes in predictive analytics and machine learning and enjoys applying her knowledge to solve real-world data challenges.

Ntsika has developed several AI-driven projects and actively participates in AI hackathons and data analysis competitions through the university’s Tech Society. She hopes to contribute to the field of AI by developing intelligent systems that drive innovation.

When she’s not coding or playing chess, she enjoys reading about AI advancements and experimenting with machine learning algorithms.
