Background & Biography

Khanyisile Mazibuko is a third-year Informatics student at the University of Pretoria with a keen interest in user experience (UX) design and front-end development.

She is passionate about creating visually appealing and user-friendly digital interfaces. Through her work on various design projects, she has developed a strong focus on accessibility and inclusivity in technology. As an active participant in the university’s Tech Society, she collaborates on app development projects and attends UX workshops. Khanyisile hopes to pursue a career in UX design and front-end engineering.

Hobbies & Interests

Outside of her studies, Khanyisile is an avid dancer who enjoys contemporary and hip-hop dance styles.

She believes that dance enhances her creativity and spatial awareness, which benefit her design work. In her free time, she loves sketching digital illustrations and exploring the latest design trends in technology.
