Background & Biography

Lesedi Magondo is a third-year Informatics student at the University of Pretoria, originally from Pretoria. She developed a strong interest in software development and data analysis early on, constantly seeking out new programming languages and frameworks to explore. Lesedi has worked on various personal projects, including mobile apps and web platforms, which have allowed her to apply her knowledge to real-world challenges.

Throughout her academic journey, Lesedi has demonstrated a passion for technology and a drive for excellence. She is an active member of the university’s Tech Society, where she collaborates with other students to create innovative solutions. Lesedi aspires to pursue a career in software engineering and contribute to the creation of cutting-edge technologies that will shape the future.

Hobbies & Interests

Outside of her academic work, Lesedi is an enthusiastic runner. She regularly participates in local races and enjoys training in the scenic areas around Pretoria. Lesedi finds that running helps her clear her mind, offering a healthy balance to her technology-focused studies.

Lesedi is also passionate about photography and enjoys capturing the beauty of everyday life. She believes that photography enhances her attention to detail and creativity, which are essential skills in both software development and data analysis. In her free time, Lesedi loves exploring new technologies, attending tech meetups, and experimenting with new programming languages and tools.
